Beagle Dog Breed

The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger Foxhound. The Beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare.

Trivia

  1. Life Span: 10-15 years
  2. Temperament: Intelligent, Even Tempered, Amiable, Determined, Gentle, Excitable
  3. Height: Male: 36–41 cm, Female: 33–38 cm
  4. Weight: Male: 10–11 kg, Female: 9–10 kg
  5. Colors: White & Tan, Lemon & White, Tri-color, Chocolate Tri, White & Chocolate, Orange & White
